Discussion Topics for the HTML 2.0 Review

This is an attempt by the editor of the specification to organize the review comments in the Topic/Proposal/Argument structure as used in the W3 Interactive Discussion application.

The materials here are gathered from:

  • the HTML 2.0 review archive maintained by Rondal Daniel at LANL
  • the www-html archive maintained by jay@eit.com
